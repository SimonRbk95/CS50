sort1 uses: Bubble Sort
How do you know?: It is the fastest algorithm when used for sorted files and faster than sort3.
sort2 uses: Merge Sort
How do you know?: It is consistently fast in all text files and the fastest in all but the sorted ones, where bubble sort takes the stage.
sort3 uses: Selection Sort
How do you know?: It is consitently slow and the slowest out of the three for sorted files.
